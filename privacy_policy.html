Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

English Club ("we," "us," or "our") operates the English Club mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

User Account Data

We collect information you provide when you register for an account, such as your name, email address, student/admin ID, and password. This information is used to authenticate you and manage your access to the app's features.

Usage Data

We collect data on how you interact with the Service, including:

  • Quiz Results: Scores, answers, and progress in vocabulary and story quizzes.
  • Library Activity: Records of books borrowed, returned, and reading history.
  • App Activity: Features used, time spent, and interactions within the app.

Device Permissions

The app may request access to certain device features to function correctly:

  • Storage: To upload/download files (e.g., Excel sheets for student data, book covers) and cache data.
  • Camera (Optional): Used for scanning QR codes for book borrowing/returning or updating profile pictures/book covers.
  • Notifications: To send reminders about book returns, new quizzes, or announcements.
  • Internet & Network State: To connect to our servers for real-time data synchronization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Management: To facilitate book borrowing, quiz administration, and student progress tracking.
  • Communication: To send you updates, notifications, and administrative messages.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the functionality and user experience of the app.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

This Service is intended for educational use. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 without verified parental or school consent. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
